Services

We’ll choose what fits your needs, goals, and clinical context.

Psychiatric evaluation

A careful assessment to understand symptoms, history, and what’s driving your experience — so we can decide next steps with confidence.

  • Clear diagnostic perspective
  • Risk & safety planning when needed
  • Personalized treatment options

Medication management

Thoughtful prescribing with a focus on tolerability, effectiveness, and the smallest helpful change — monitored with consistency.

  • Evidence-informed options
  • Side-effect monitoring
  • Collaborative decisions

Therapy-informed care

For many people, medication alone isn’t the whole answer. We integrate skills, structure, and practical tools (CBT-informed) into the plan.

  • Tools for anxiety & mood
  • Behavior & routine support
  • Better sleep & focus habits

FAQ

Short, clear answers to common questions.

How long is a session?

Most follow-up visits are about 50 minutes. Initial evaluations may be longer depending on complexity.

Do you offer telepsychiatry?

Yes. Telepsychiatry is available for California-based clients when clinically appropriate.

Will I definitely need medication?

Not necessarily. We’ll review options and decide together. Some people benefit from therapy-informed care, lifestyle changes, and structured support without medication.

What if I need to reschedule?

Please reach out as early as possible so we can offer the time to someone else and find a better slot for you.
