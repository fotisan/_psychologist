Services

Medication should feel supportive, not disruptive. We choose the simplest effective option, monitor how you feel, and adjust with care.

FAQ

Clear answers, so you know what to expect.

How long are visits?

Most visits are about 50 minutes. First visits can be longer when we need more time to understand the full picture.

Do you offer video visits?

Yes. Video visits are available for California based clients when appropriate for your situation.

Do I have to take medication?

Not always. We look at options together. For some people, skills, structure, and lifestyle changes are enough. For others, medication can make the work easier.

Need to reschedule?

Just let us know as soon as you can. We will help you find a better time, and open the slot for someone who needs it.
