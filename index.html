About

Sometimes it’s not one "big" event that brings us here. It’s the accumulation. The fatigue that stays, the tension in the body, the thoughts running the same track.

Therapy, for me, is a safe harbor. A space where you can be heard with respect, without needing to prove anything. We work with what is true for you, in a way that can live within your daily life.

The goal isn't perfection. It is support, understanding, and a steady collaboration so that life feels calmer, more predictable, and more human.

How long are visits?

Typical duration is 50 minutes. This time is exclusively yours, to talk without rushing.

Do you offer video visits?

Yes. For California residents, secure video visits are available when appropriate.

Do I have to take medication?

Not always. We look at options together. For some, skills and lifestyle changes are enough.
